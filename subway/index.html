06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Dark Tunnel Backgrounds

Neutral grey without warm or cool bias, like underground station walls and platform tiles.

2

Transit Yellow Accent

The universal metro wayfinding color marks key interactive elements and countdown timers.

3

Route-Color Semantics

Green (uptown/go), orange (crosstown/transfer), red (express/alert), blue (downtown/info) map to transit lines.

4

Transport Sans Headings

Overpass channels the clean, functional style of transit wayfinding signage systems.

5

Route Stripe Hero

8px top bar with all four line colors establishes the transit identity immediately.

6

Transit Components

Station signs, arrival boards, turnstile dividers, and transfer badges bring the subway experience into the design.

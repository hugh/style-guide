06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Aged Parchment

Warm cream-tan backgrounds evoke antique paper and leather-bound volumes.

2

Wax Seal Accent

Rich burgundy marks key elements like a pressed seal on correspondence.

3

Ornate Serif Typography

Playfair Display italic headings with Lora body create period elegance worthy of a Victorian parlour.

4

Sharp Formal Edges

2px border-radius maintains a crisp, institutional feel throughout all components.

5

Victorian Semantics

Ivy green (approved), antique gold (craft), wax red (urgent), royal blue (authority).

6

Ornamental Motifs

Wax seals, ribbon banners, medallions, and filigree dividers bring period decoration into the design language.

7

Decorative Rules

Ornamental gradient lines frame the hero section, evoking engraved page borders.

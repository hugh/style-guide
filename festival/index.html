06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Dark Purple-Black Backgrounds

Deep purple-black tones like the night sky above an outdoor festival, creating maximum contrast for vivid colors.

2

Psychedelic Gradients

Multi-color gradient overlays on the hero and lineup poster give that classic psychedelic festival poster energy.

3

Bold Poster Typography

Bebas Neue delivers the tall, condensed, uppercase impact of real concert poster lettering.

4

Four-Color Energy

Electric pink, sunset orange, vivid purple, and bright cyan create vibrant color clashes that work through sheer energy.

5

Festival Identity Components

Wristband badges, ticket stub dividers, lineup posters, and stage cards bring the live music experience into the design.

6

Schedule-Ready Mono

IBM Plex Mono handles times, labels, and wristband text with the functional clarity needed for set times and gate info.

06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Warm Wood Backgrounds

Light wood tones from raw pine to aged walnut. Every surface feels like sanded wood, honest and warm.

2

Rich Walnut Accent

The deep walnut stain marks primary interactive elements, headings, and key components throughout.

3

Wood Species Semantics

Pine green (fresh/go), maple honey (warm/refine), cherry red (alert/execute), steel blue (tool/info) map to workshop materials.

4

Slab Serif Headings

Bitter channels the sturdy, reliable feel of a workshop sign — built to last, easy to read at a glance.

5

Sturdy 2px Borders

All borders are 2px solid with minimal radius — like sanded wood edges, suggesting honest joinery and construction.

6

Workshop Components

Blueprint cards, dovetail dividers, lumber stamps, and tool racks bring the craftsman's workshop into the design system.

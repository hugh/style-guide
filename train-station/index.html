Platform 05 — Design Principles

Key Patterns

1

Warm Dark Surfaces

Brown-black backgrounds (#1c1a17) with a warm undertone — like aged wood panelling in a grand terminus waiting room.

2

Brass & Iron

Two material accents instead of a single primary colour. Warm brass for headings, platform numbers, and primary emphasis. Cool iron for secondary and structural elements.

3

Railway Signal Colours

Red, green, and amber taken directly from signal lamp colours. Used semantically: green for on-time/good, amber for delays/caution, red for cancellations/disruption.

4

Expanded Station-Sign Type

Stint Ultra Expanded has a naturally wide letterform that evokes cast-metal station signs and engraved brass plates. Body text uses the clean Libre Franklin.

5

Departures Board

The signature unique component. Grid-based timetable display with time, destination, status, and platform columns — like a split-flap arrivals/departures indicator.

6

Zero Radius, Sharp Corners

No border-radius anywhere. Strictly rectilinear — like cast iron, riveted steel, and platform edge tiles. Ornamental dividers provide the softness.

04 — Protocols

Engineering Principles

Use the principle list for ordered items with detailed descriptions.

1

Measure Twice, Cut Once

Every precision component must be verified against the blueprint before machining begins. Tolerances of 0.02mm or less are standard.

2

Pressure Before Speed

The boiler pressure must reach nominal operating range before engaging the main drive assembly.

3

Maintain the Patina

Copper oxidation is a feature, not a defect. The verdigris patina protects underlying metal from further corrosion.

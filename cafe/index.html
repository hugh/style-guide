Chalkboard Cafe

A warm, inviting design system inspired by cafe chalkboard menus — hand-lettered chalk headings, latte brown accents, menu boards with dotted price lines, and coffee ring dividers for single-page HTML documents.

06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

These patterns define the visual identity of the Cafe system.

1

Slate Green Chalkboard

Backgrounds use dark green-tinted tones like a real chalkboard, not cold greys or pure blacks. This creates warmth and familiarity.

2

Latte Brown Accent

The primary accent is a warm latte brown inspired by steamed milk and wooden countertops. Cozy, inviting, and appetizing.

3

Cafe Semantics

Four semantic colors map to cafe concepts: latte brown (warmth), pastry pink (sweet), espresso red (bold), herbal teal (fresh).

4

Chalk Lettering

Kalam gives headings the feel of hand-lettered chalkboard menus, slightly imperfect and full of character, like a barista’s handwriting.

5

Clean Body Text

DM Sans provides clear readability for body content, like printed menus and cafe signage that complement the handwritten board.

6

Cafe Artifacts

Menu boards, daily specials, coffee ring stains, and order tickets bring the physical experience of visiting a cafe into the design.

7

Chalk Dust Texture

The hero section uses faint radial gradients and ruled lines evoking the dusty, smudged surface of a well-used chalkboard.

Design System Reference

Brutalist Web

An anti-design system that rejects decoration. System fonts, zero border-radius, thick black borders, no transitions. Raw concrete aesthetics for single-page HTML documents.

06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Raw Concrete

Flat grey backgrounds. No gradients, no warmth, no texture overlays. The surface is what it is.

2

Construction Orange

One loud accent color. It demands attention like a safety vest on a building site. Everything else is black, white, and grey.

3

System Fonts Only

Arial. Times New Roman. Courier. No imports. No web fonts. The deliberate mismatch between serif body and sans headings is intentional.

4

Zero Border-Radius

Every corner is sharp. No rounded corners, ever. No exceptions. The rawness is the aesthetic.

5

No Hover Transitions

Nothing moves. Nothing fades. Nothing lifts. Everything is static and monolithic. The weight is the point.

6

Thick Black Borders

3-4px pure black on every component. The structure is exposed, not hidden behind subtle hairlines.

7

Jarring Scale

Hero text at 8rem. Body in Times New Roman at 1rem. The contrast between massive and modest is deliberate discomfort.

8

Industrial Artifacts

Monolith blocks, exposed grids, hazard stripes, and stencil stamps. The construction site is the metaphor.

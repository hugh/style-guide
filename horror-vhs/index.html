06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

CRT Black Backgrounds

Near-black surfaces with no warm or cool bias, like the dead screen of an unpowered cathode ray tube.

2

Night-Vision Green Accent

The signature colour of infrared camera footage drives primary accent, links, and interactive states.

3

Four-Signal Semantics

Green (night-vision/detection), amber (timestamp/data), red (REC/alert), blue (static/info) map to found-footage concepts.

4

VCR Typography

VT323 channels the on-screen display of 1990s camcorders. All-caps, pixelated edges, monospaced rhythm.

5

Scanline & Vignette Overlay

Body pseudo-elements add repeating scanlines and a dark-corner vignette across the entire page.

6

Sharp CRT Edges

Zero border-radius on all components. Every corner is a hard right angle, like a video signal boundary.

05 — Lesson Cards

This Week’s Lessons

Lesson 14
Mon 23 Feb 2026
Objective

Students will identify the major biomes of Earth and describe their climate characteristics.

We begin our ecology unit with an overview of terrestrial biomes. Students will work in pairs to research one biome and present findings to the class.

  • Tropical rainforest — warm and wet year-round
  • Desert — extreme temperatures, minimal precipitation
  • Tundra — permafrost, short growing season
  • Temperate forest — seasonal variation, deciduous trees
Lesson 15
Wed 25 Feb 2026
Objective

Explain the flow of energy through an ecosystem using food chains and food webs.

Building on the biome presentations, we explore how energy moves from producers to consumers. Students will construct a food web for a local ecosystem.

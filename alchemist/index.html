03 — Layout

Structure

Documents use a single-column layout with a centered container. Sections stack vertically with generous spacing against the dark parchment background.

📜

Container

Max-width 900px, centered, with 2rem horizontal padding.

⚙️

Sections

5rem vertical padding. Separated by 1px solid border-top.

↔️

Full-Bleed

Diagrams can break out to 1100px max-width with the full-bleed class.

📱

Responsive

Fluid hero type with clamp(). Card grids collapse to 1 column below 640px.

Section Pattern

Every section follows the same structure: a monospace label in the format 01 — Label, followed by an h2 heading, then body content and components.

06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

These patterns define the visual identity of the Alchemist system.

1

Dark Parchment Backgrounds

Near-black backgrounds with warm brown undertones create the feel of aged vellum viewed by candlelight.

2

Gold Leaf Accent

The primary accent is a rich gold that evokes gilt illumination on manuscript pages. It marks headings, callout borders, and principle numbers.

3

Alchemical Semantics

Four semantic colors map to classical substances: viridian green (growth/verdant), gold (transmutation/value), cinnabar red (mercury/danger), lapis blue (wisdom/knowledge).

4

Sharp Carved Corners

4px border-radius on all components evokes the precise edges of carved stone tablets and manuscript borders.

5

Scholarly Serif Typography

Cinzel for ornamental headings channels Roman inscriptional lettering. Crimson Pro for body text provides comfortable reading with a manuscript quality.

6

Mystical Unique Components

Drop caps, recipe cards, transmutation circles, manuscript dividers, and sigil badges bring alchemical laboratory aesthetics into the design language.

7

Illuminated Hero

The hero section features a subtle radial gold glow and an ornamental circle, evoking the luminous quality of gilt manuscript pages.

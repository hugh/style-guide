05 — Unique Components

Folded Card

A card with a folded corner triangle in the top-right and a thin accent stripe. The fold creates a subtle shadow, evoking a turned-down page corner. Add semantic classes for color: capture, refine, execute, batch.

Default Folded Card

Origami pink accent stripe with the signature folded corner. Content sits cleanly beneath the fold with a subtle paper shadow offset.

Capture Variant

Sage green stripe — use for collection and input stages.

Batch Variant

Sky blue stripe — use for processing and batch operations.

Crane Marker

A decorative section separator featuring a geometric origami crane SVG centered between thin horizontal rules. Place between major content sections.

The crane SVG uses faceted polygons in surface tones with an accent-colored beak.

Crease Divider

A delicate horizontal divider that looks like a paper crease — a thin line with a subtle shadow on one side, as if light is hitting a real fold. Much more refined than a standard <hr>.

The crease uses a gradient shadow below the line to simulate depth at a paper fold.

Fortune Teller Badge

An inline badge shaped like a paper fortune teller (cootie catcher) viewed from above — a diamond with four colored quadrants. Text label centered.

06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Clean Paper Backgrounds

White and light grey surfaces layered like folded sheets. Each surface level is a slightly deeper fold shadow.

2

Origami Pink Accent

Traditional origami paper red-pink marks key elements, accents, and the folded card stripe.

3

Crisp Geometric Edges

Border-radius 0px everywhere. Folded paper creates clean geometric shapes with no rounding.

4

Paper Fold Shadows

Subtle offset box-shadows suggest sheets of paper layered and folded, creating gentle depth.

5

Friendly Rounded Headings

Nunito brings a soft, approachable geometric quality that contrasts with the sharp edges of the containers.

6

Paper Color Semantics

Sage green, gold foil, traditional red, and sky blue map to different origami paper types, maintaining consistent visual meaning.

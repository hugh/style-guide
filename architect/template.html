04 — Specifications

Design Principles

Use the principle list for ordered items with detailed descriptions.

1

Orientation & Light

Primary living spaces face south to maximize passive solar gain. Deep overhangs control summer heat while admitting low-angle winter light.

2

Material Honesty

Expressed structure and natural materials. Concrete, timber, and glass are used in their raw state without applied finishes where possible.

3

Threshold Sequence

A deliberate progression from public to private spaces through a series of spatial thresholds, creating a sense of arrival and discovery.

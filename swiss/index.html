06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Pure White Canvas

Start from absolute white. Every mark on the page must earn its place. No textures, no patterns, no noise.

2

Single Accent Color

Swiss red (#e20000) is the only chromatic element. It marks structure — rule lines, numbers, emphasis — never decoration.

3

Typographic Unity

One typeface family (Inter) at multiple weights creates hierarchy. Unity of form, variety of expression.

4

Grid Discipline

All content aligns to a strict grid. Asymmetric placement within the grid creates dynamic compositions.

5

Hairline Borders

1px solid grey lines provide structure without visual weight. No shadows, no rounded corners, no gradients.

6

Generous Whitespace

Space between elements is as carefully considered as the elements themselves. Let the content breathe.

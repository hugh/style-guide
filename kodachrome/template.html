04 — Technical Data

Film Stock Reference

1

Meter Carefully

Slide film has approximately 5 stops of dynamic range. Expose for highlights and let shadows go deep.

2

Shoot in Good Light

Kodachrome renders best in direct sunlight or golden hour. Overcast conditions produce muted results.

3

Store Properly

Keep unexposed film refrigerated and processed slides in archival sleeves away from humidity and UV light.

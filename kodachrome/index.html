06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Warm Cream Backgrounds

Aged photo paper tones with golden undertones, like prints on a drying line or slides on a light table.

2

Kodachrome Red Accent

The iconic saturated warm red marks key interactive elements and primary emphasis throughout the system.

3

Film Color Semantics

Green (vegetation/go), yellow (sunlight/process), red (warmth/alert), blue (sky/archive) map to Kodachrome’s signature rendition.

4

Mid-Century Geometric Headings

Libre Franklin channels the clean, confident style of 1960s–70s film packaging and camera manuals.

5

Warm Serif Body

Source Serif 4 provides an intimate, readable warmth befitting personal photo essays and nostalgic storytelling.

6

Analog Film Components

Slide mounts, sprocket strips, contact sheets, and light leak badges bring the tangible darkroom-to-projector experience into the design.

Design System Reference

The Cartographer

An aged-parchment design system inspired by antique maps and surveyor's notebooks — topographic contour patterns, coordinate grids, legend boxes, compass-rose dividers, and location cards for single-page HTML documents.

03 — Layout

Structure

Documents use a single-column layout with a centered container. Sections stack vertically with generous spacing and fine-line borders like map panel edges.

📜

Container

Max-width 900px, centered, with 2rem horizontal padding.

📌

Sections

5rem vertical padding. Separated by 1px solid border-top.

↔️

Full-Bleed

Diagrams can break out to 1100px max-width with the full-bleed class.

📱

Responsive

Fluid hero type with clamp(). Card grids collapse to 1 column below 640px.

Section Pattern

Every section follows the same structure: a monospace label in the format 01 — Label, followed by an h2 heading, then body content and components.

06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

These patterns define the visual identity of the Cartographer system.

1

Aged Parchment

Backgrounds use warm cream-tan tones like hand-drawn maps on aged paper, unfolded under warm light.

2

Burnt Umber Accent

The primary accent evokes sepia ink and hand-coloured cartographic details found in antique surveys.

3

Cartographic Semantics

Four semantic colors map to geographic concepts: forest green (vegetation), goldenrod (elevation), terra cotta (boundaries), cartographic blue (water).

4

Old-Map Typography

Cormorant Garamond delivers the elegance of engraved map cartouches. Source Serif 4 provides crisp body text readability.

5

Sharp Map Edges

3px border-radius keeps edges subtle and precise, like the crisp folds and panel lines of a folded survey map.

6

Contour-Line Texture

Concentric elliptical gradients in the hero and wavy SVG dividers evoke the topographic contour lines found on ordnance survey maps.

7

Map Artifacts

Legend boxes, compass roses, coordinate cards, and contour dividers bring the physical cartographic toolkit into the design language.

8

Coordinate Precision

IBM Plex Mono renders coordinates, grid references, and elevation data with the technical precision of survey instruments.

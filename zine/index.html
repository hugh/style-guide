05 — Unique Components

Tape Strip Card

A card that looks taped to the page. Semi-transparent tape strips at the top corners, slight rotation, and a torn-paper bottom edge via clip-path.

This card is taped on

Notice the scotch tape at the top corners, the slight rotation, and the torn bottom edge. Everything about this component screams "cut out of a magazine and stuck to a page." Use it for featured content, quotes, or anything that needs to feel physically present.

Another taped clipping

Rotate them differently for variety. Each one should feel like it was hastily slapped on at a different angle. The tape strips use semi-transparent yellow backgrounds with subtle borders.

Ransom Note Block

Each word/phrase alternates between different font styles and sizes via :nth-child selectors. The collage effect of mixed typography creates the classic cut-and-paste ransom note aesthetic. Wrap each word or phrase in a <span>.

We reject your polished corporate templates and demand raw chaos in every pixel because design should be dangerous not safe and never boring

Xerox Divider

A horizontal divider that looks like a degraded photocopy line. Dark grainy bar with uneven edges created by a clip-path with jagged polygon points.

Use <div class="xerox-divider"></div> between sections or content blocks to add that photocopier artifact aesthetic.

06 — Key Patterns

Design Principles

1

Newsprint Backgrounds

Off-white yellowed paper tones, layered like torn sheets pasted on top of each other. Never use pure white.

2

Bold Black Borders

Thick 2-3px black borders on everything. No soft edges, no subtle borders. Marker-weight ink lines.

3

Zero Border Radius

Nothing is rounded. Everything is torn, cut, and sharp. The only softness comes from the tape strips.

4

Mixed Typography

Special Elite for headings (typewriter), Permanent Marker for callouts (handwritten), Inter for body (readable contrast), IBM Plex Mono for labels.

5

Slight Rotations

Elements should look hand-placed. Subtle 1-3 degree rotations on cards, callouts, stickers. Nothing is perfectly aligned.

6

DIY Punk Energy

The overall feel should be anarchic and handmade. Tape strips, torn edges, xerox artifacts, ransom-note text. Every element tells you someone made this with scissors and a photocopier.

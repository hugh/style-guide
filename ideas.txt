Noir
  Film noir / case file. High-contrast black and white, single red accent.
  Typewriter font (Courier Prime), manila folder card backgrounds, evidence
  tag components, redacted text blocks (black bars over text), crime board
  string-connection diagrams, stamped "CLASSIFIED" labels.

Journalist
  Newspaper broadsheet. Cream newsprint background, multi-column layouts,
  large drop caps, serif-heavy (like a front page), dateline headers, pull
  quote components, byline blocks, "BREAKING" callout banners, thin column
  rules.

Botanist
  Field journal. Warm cream/linen background, earthy greens and warm browns,
  specimen card components (bordered cards with taxonomy labels), pressed-
  flower muted palette, field note callouts with date/location stamps,
  classification tables.

Librarian
  Card catalog warmth. Rich cream, dark walnut brown, gold accents, card-
  catalog-style index cards as components, Dewey decimal section numbering
  (025.4 — Reference), bookplate-style headers, library stamp marks,
  due-date grid tables.

Manga / Anime
  Speed lines, screentone dot patterns (finer than halftone), dramatic
  diagonal compositions, condensed sans-serif type, selective color pops
  on high-contrast black & white. Pink/magenta accent instead of red.
  Japanese-inspired layout with panels and gutters.

Dark Vigilante
  Batman/Daredevil mood. Near-black backgrounds, one piercing accent
  color (electric blue or blood red), sharp angular shapes, gothic serif
  headings. Brooding and atmospheric rather than fun. Rain/fog texture
  overlays, case-file-style components.

Modern Cinematic / MCU
  Dark and sleek. Near-black backgrounds with metallic gradients,
  glowing edge accents, subtle light-leak effects. Clean geometric
  sans-serif. Movie title sequence feel rather than printed page.
  Widescreen aspect-ratio hero panels.

Cyberpunk / Neon
  Rain-soaked dark streets. Near-black backgrounds with hot pink and
  electric cyan neon accents, glitch/distortion effects on hover,
  CJK-mixed typography fragments as decorative elements. Monospace
  or condensed grotesque body text, futuristic geometric headings.
  Neon sign glow on borders (box-shadow blur), rain-streak overlay
  textures, corrupted-data callout blocks, neural-link connection
  diagrams, holographic shimmer gradients on cards.

Art Nouveau
  Organic flowing curves and botanical elegance. Muted jewel tones —
  deep teal, dusty rose, antique gold, sage green — on cream or soft
  ivory backgrounds. Mucha-style decorative borders with sinuous
  vine/tendril frames, ornamental header cartouches, flowing hairline
  rules. Elegant serif headings (like Playfair Display), clean serif
  body. Floral corner ornaments on cards, stained-glass panel dividers,
  medallion section markers with organic motifs.

Vaporwave
  Nostalgic retro-futurism. Pastel pink-to-purple gradient backgrounds,
  cyan and hot magenta accents, chrome/silver metallic text effects.
  Receding perspective grid floor as hero background, Roman bust and
  palm tree decorative elements, glitchy VHS tracking-line dividers,
  Windows 95-style beveled card borders. Japanese text fragments as
  decorative labels, sunset gradient bars, marble texture surfaces,
  soft bloom/glow on everything.

Nautical / Maritime
  Navy and brass, rope-knot dividers, porthole-framed cards, signal
  flag tag components, lighthouse beacon loading indicator, ship's
  log callout blocks, compass bearing section markers, canvas/sail
  texture backgrounds. Deep ocean navy base, warm brass/gold accents,
  weathered rope tan secondary. Fonts: strong slab serif for headings,
  clean sans for body, monospace for coordinates/log entries.

Apothecary / Pharmacy
  Vintage medicine cabinet aesthetic, dark bottle-green and amber,
  prescription label cards with Rx headers, dosage tables, mortar-
  and-pestle section markers, vintage label typography (small caps
  serif), medicine bottle silhouette dividers, ingredient lists with
  quantity grids. Aged label paper surfaces, hand-lettered serif feel.

Steampunk
  Brass and copper on dark leather, gear/cog decorative elements,
  pressure gauge indicators, Victorian mechanical typeface headings,
  pneumatic tube callout containers, rivet-studded panel borders,
  telegraph ticker tape dividers, clockwork animation on hover. Deep
  brown leather base, warm brass/copper accents. Fonts: ornate
  Victorian serif for headings, clean serif body, mono for gauges.

Tarot / Occult
  Deep indigo and midnight purple backgrounds, gold-foil accents,
  celestial star-field textures. Tarot card components with ornate
  frames, zodiac wheel section markers, crystal ball callout panels,
  mystic symbol dividers. Serif headings with an arcane, woodcut feel.
  Fonts: decorative serif (like Cinzel Decorative) for headings, clean
  serif for body, mono for card numerals.

Expedition / Explorer's Journal
  Sun-bleached canvas backgrounds, weathered leather brown and compass
  brass accents. Field sketch cards with hand-drawn borders, topographic
  elevation callouts, expedition log entries with date/coordinates,
  rope-lash dividers. Handwritten heading font with clean serif body.
  Fonts: handwriting (like Caveat or Patrick Hand) for headings, clean
  serif for body, mono for coordinates.

Neon Diner / Roadside
  Near-black asphalt backgrounds with hot neon tube accents in pink,
  teal, and amber. Menu board card components, "OPEN 24HRS" blinking
  sign callouts, chrome jukebox progress bars, checkered floor pattern
  dividers. Retro script headings with condensed sans body. Fonts:
  retro script (like Pacifico or Sacramento) for headings, condensed
  sans for body, mono for prices/labels.

Medieval Manuscript / Illuminated
  Aged vellum cream backgrounds, rich vermillion and ultramarine accents
  with gold leaf. Ornamental drop caps, illuminated initial letter
  components, marginalia annotation callouts, vine-tendril border frames,
  chapter heading cartouches. Blackletter display headings with clean
  serif body. Fonts: blackletter (like UnifrakturCook) for headings,
  clean serif for body, mono for verse numbers.

Greenhouse / Terrarium
  Warm humid greens on dark loam backgrounds. Terracotta and moss
  accents, botanical identification cards with Latin names, humidity
  gauge progress bars, plant care tag components, propagation timeline
  dividers, seed packet callout panels. Organic rounded shapes with
  earthy textures. Fonts: rounded sans for headings, clean sans for
  body, mono for measurements.

Pirate / Treasure Map
  Burnt parchment with charcoal ink and gold doubloon accents. Treasure
  chest card components, X-marks-the-spot section markers, torn-edge
  paper borders, ship's log callout entries with coordinates, compass
  rose dividers, plank-walk pipeline stages. Weathered and adventurous.
  Fonts: decorative serif (like Pirata One) for headings, clean serif
  for body, mono for coordinates.

Space Station / ISS
  Clean white module interiors with technical grey panels and status-
  light accents (green/amber/red). Modular habitat card components,
  life support gauge bars, crew manifest tables, airlock status
  indicators, orbital window viewport frames, mission elapsed time
  counters. Clinical and functional. Fonts: clean geometric sans for
  headings, clean sans for body, mono for telemetry.

Aquarium / Fish Tank
  Deep blue-black water backgrounds, soft caustic light ripples, neon
  fish-color accents (clownfish orange, tang blue, anemone pink). Glass
  panel card frames with refraction edges, water parameter gauge bars
  (pH, temp, salinity), species info cards with silhouette icons, bubble
  column dividers, feeding schedule timers. Fonts: rounded sans for
  headings, clean sans for body, mono for measurements.

Speakeasy / Prohibition
  Dark mahogany and smoke-grey, art deco gold trim, whiskey amber
  accents. Password card entry components, cocktail recipe cards with
  ingredient lists, "Members Only" stamped callouts, cigarette smoke
  wisp dividers, bootlegger ledger tables, jazz-age script headings.
  Fonts: art deco display (like Poiret One) for headings, elegant
  sans for body, mono for ledger entries.

Operating Theatre / Surgical
  Scrub green backgrounds, clinical steel borders, vital signs monitor
  components with ECG wave dividers, surgical checklist cards, X-ray
  lightbox image containers, triage priority tags (red/yellow/green),
  sterile field panel frames. Fonts: clean geometric sans for headings,
  clean sans for body, mono for vitals readouts.

Toybox / Building Blocks
  Bright primary backgrounds with thick black outlines, snap-together
  brick card components, instruction manual step numbering, toy packaging
  window-box frames, sticker sheet tag collections, wind-up key loading
  indicators. Fonts: rounded bold sans for headings, clean sans for
  body, mono for part numbers.

Botanical Garden / Herbarium
  Pressed-flower muted palette on dark forest backgrounds. Herbarium
  specimen sheet cards with collection data labels, garden map grid
  containers, bloom calendar timelines, seed dispersal SVG diagrams,
  potting soil dark texture surfaces. Fonts: elegant serif for headings,
  clean sans for body, mono for taxonomy/dates.

Radio Station / FM Broadcast
  Dark studio backgrounds with on-air red and frequency dial accents.
  Equalizer bar components, playlist rotation cards, broadcast log
  tables, "ON AIR" illuminated sign callouts, frequency spectrum
  dividers, request line ticker components. Fonts: bold condensed sans
  for headings, clean sans for body, mono for frequencies/timestamps.

Observatory / Astronomy
  Deep midnight blue-black with nebula purple and stellar white accents.
  Star chart grid containers, constellation diagram SVGs, celestial
  coordinate callouts, observation log cards with RA/Dec data, light
  pollution gauge bars, telescope aperture circular frames. Fonts:
  clean geometric sans for headings, clean sans for body, mono for
  coordinates.

Haunted House / Gothic Horror
  Deep charcoal and mildew grey backgrounds, flickering candle amber
  and blood crimson accents. Cobweb corner ornaments on cards, creaking
  door panel transitions, tombstone-shaped section markers, spirit
  board letter callouts, cracked mirror dividers, fog-drift overlay
  textures. Fonts: gothic serif (like Nosifer or Creepster) for
  headings, clean serif body, mono for dates/epitaphs.

Wrestling / Lucha Libre
  Hot bold backgrounds with neon accent combos (lime green + hot pink,
  electric blue + orange). Mask-shaped card frames, championship belt
  score components, ring rope dividers, entrance ramp pipeline stages,
  match card poster callouts with VS badges. Maximum energy and drama.
  Fonts: impact/condensed bold for headings, clean sans for body,
  mono for stats.

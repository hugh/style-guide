01 — The Prophecy

The Words of the Oracle

I

n ages past, when the world was young and magic flowed freely through every brook and bough, the Oracle of the Crystal Tower spoke of a time when five realms would be united. Each realm held a fragment of the ancient enchantment, and only when brought together would the kingdom be whole once more.

The Five Fragments

The prophecy speaks of five fragments, each guarded by a creature of immense power. To gather them all requires courage, wisdom, compassion, and an unwavering heart.

🌲

The Emerald Shard

Hidden deep within the Enchanted Forest, guarded by the ancient Treant of Mosshollow Glen.

👑

The Golden Crest

Locked within the royal treasury, protected by riddles only the worthy can solve.

🍎

The Ruby Heart

Buried beneath the Dragon's Keep, where fire and stone meet at the mountain's core.

✨

The Amethyst Eye

Suspended in the Court Wizard's sanctum, visible only under the light of a full moon.

Ancient Warning: The fragments must be gathered in the correct order, lest the enchantment turn against the bearer. Begin with the forest, end with the stars.

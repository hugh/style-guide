04 — Techniques

Baking Principles

Use the principle list for ordered items with detailed descriptions.

1

Mise en Place

Measure and prepare all ingredients before you start. Room-temperature butter and eggs produce the best texture.

2

Don't Overmix

Fold gently once dry ingredients are added. Overworking develops gluten and makes baked goods tough.

3

Know Your Oven

Use an oven thermometer. Most ovens run 10–25 degrees off from the dial setting.
